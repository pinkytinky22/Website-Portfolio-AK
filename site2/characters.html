    Overview

        Here is a list containing some of the main characters from the series. Not all characters are listed but the characters that appear the most are. You can even chose whether you like the character or not!


    Jerrica Benton

        Jerrica Benton is the practical, hardworking manager and owner of the Starlight Foundation and co-owner of Starlight Music alongside her younger sister, Kimber. Her alter-ego is the rock star, Jem, the lead singer of the The Holograms and one of the main reasons why the Starlight Foundation is long standing against Eric Raymond. With the help of Synergy Jerrica is able to turn into Jem with just one line, "Showtime Synergy."

    Jem

        Jem is the leader/main vocalist of the famous rock group The Holograms, but in reality, she's the alter ego of Starlight Records executive Jerrica Benton. The Jem identity is created via holographic technology through the use of a pair of special earrings which they named 'The Jem Star Earrings'. Jerrica inherited them from her late father. Only a few people know her secret, mainly the rest of her bandmates. Jem also changes back to Jerrica by saying, "Show's over Synergy."
