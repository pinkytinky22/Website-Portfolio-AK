Overview

    Jem and the Holograms is an American animated musical television series that ran from 1985 to 1988. The series was a joint collaboration by Hasbro, Sunbrow Productions and Marvel Productions, the same team responsible for G.I. Joe and Transformers. The animation for most of the episodes was provided by Japanese animaiton studio Toei Animation, including the opening sequence provided by South Korean studio AKOM.
