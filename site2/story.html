    • Synopsis

        The show follows Jerrica Benton, who transforms into the glamorous rock star Jem with the help of holographic technology. Along with her band, The Holograms, Jem navigates the music industry, rival bands, and personal challenges. Combining adventure, music, and drama, the series became iconic for its strong female characters and catchy musical performances.


    Season 1

        The first season of Jem aired between November 3, 1985, and March 15, 1987. The season has a total of 26 episodes each with a running time of approximately 22 minutes. The first 5 episodes initially aired as 15 7-minute segments with each episode broken into 3 parts. There was a later version of the first 5 episodes which came out in 1986 when the show first came into syndication where some scenes from the original 7 minute segments were either altered, extended (like a lot of the music videos sequences) or removed entirely.


    Season 2

        The second season of Jem aired between September 21, 1987, and January 12, 1988.


    Season 3

        The third and final season of Jem aired between February 2, 1988, and May 2, 1988.
