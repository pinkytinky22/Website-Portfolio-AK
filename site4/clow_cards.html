Overview

Counting the Clow Cards there are the nineteen (in the manga) or fifty-three (in the anime). They are magical, mystical cards used in the first two seasons of Cardcaptor Sakura. In the third season, the cards are transformed into Sakura Cards in order to adapt to the magic of their new master, Sakura Kinomoto.
Each Clow Card has its own personality, ranging from good-natured to violent and its own powers that are mostly centered around an elemental force or performing a specific task. When in use or acting on their own, the cards assume alternative forms that vary from card to card.
The Sakura Cards are the Clow Cards transformed by Sakura's own magic as it further develops and grows stronger.

Clow Cards and Sakura Cards

Clow Cards

Clow Cards are tall, tarot‑style cards with unified sun–moon motifs and purple‑backed illustrations of characters or objects representing each card’s power, sometimes appearing in paired “sister” designs.

Sakura Cards

When Sakura transforms the Clow Cards, they become pink, star‑themed Sakura Cards with new gold accents, a twelve‑point star on the back, and a redesigned front featuring a large star and Japanese naming. The anime keeps the original figures with pink tones, while the manga gives many cards new details, with some—like The Sword and The Shield—changing entirely.
